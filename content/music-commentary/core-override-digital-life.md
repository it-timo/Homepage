Core Override is about technology and identity, but not technology as spectacle. The album looks at routine, digital relationships, modern communication, self-awareness, and the work of taking agency back.

Used To Be Easy opens that exploration through memory, nostalgia, distance, and connection. Its video project centers on the Walker and uses warm memory silhouettes to represent remembered human connection—not ghosts, and not a generic science-fiction effect.
