Grounded Fire is a thirteen-track concept album structured in three acts: Disconnection, Reactivation, and Self Respect Through Intensity.

The sequence begins in an idle state and moves through simulated warmth, distance, pulse, voltage, and the cost of refusing mistakes. By the title track and No Script, reconnection is no longer passive. It becomes a choice to be present without reducing depth to make it easier for someone else to accept.
