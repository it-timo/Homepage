JARVIS is a personal orchestration platform intended to connect projects, memory, documentation, automation, and creative work. The unifying principle is Autonomous Until the Gate.

Automation should be able to gather context, prepare work, and advance routine processes. Consequential choices still need visible boundaries where human judgment takes responsibility. The gate is not a failure of autonomy; it is the architecture that makes autonomy trustworthy.
